# Research Dump: Granfondo Antalya (UCI Gran Fondo World Series Antalya)

## Quick Facts
- Current official name: UCI Gran Fondo World Series Antalya (formerly Nirvana Granfondo Antalya, under title sponsor Nirvana Hotels)
- 2026 edition: 6-8 November 2026, road races held 8 November — confirmed on UCI's own 2026 competition listing
- 2026 long-course vitals: 105 km / 658 m; options are 105 km Gran Fondo, 73 km Short Course, 20 km Time Trial
- Genuine UCI Gran Fondo World Series qualifier — top 25% of age-group finishers advance to the UCI Gran Fondo World Championships
- Course changes materially every documented year: 98 km/517 m (2024), 92.6 km/512 m (2025), 105 km/658 m (2026 reported)
- Start/finish: outside The Land of Legends resort in the Kadriye/Belek district east of Antalya
- 2025 long-course field: 207 starters (per official results PDF)
- Turkish Cycling Federation 2026 calendar lists only the non-Nirvana name
