## Course facts
- Two formats: 145 km competitive Race (finishes Anakkara, Idukki) and 160 km non-competitive Tour (continues to Treetop Hotel, Thekkady). Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/termsandconditions, https://www.tourofthekkady.com/faq
- Elevation: organizer states "3,000+ m", no exact GPX figure published; a 2025 event report cites ~3,200m for the 160km course. Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/about, https://www.mangalorean.com/tour-of-thekkady-2025-a-crucible-of-endurance-excellence-and-ecstasy/
- Route: Kottayam/Nagampadom -> Ettumanoor -> Pala -> Muttom -> Kulamavu Dam -> Idukki Dam/Cheruthoni -> Kattappana -> Puliyanmala -> Anakkara -> Thekkady. Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/termsandconditions, https://www.onmanorama.com/travel/kerala/2026/02/04/tour-of-thekkady-season-5-cycling-race.html
- Climbs (2025 rider account): ~4km opening climb near km32; 12km HC climb (~750m gain); 8km Cat 1 climb; major final climb from ~km127. 2026 introduced a King/Queen of Calvary Mount competition on an 8km ascent. Source: https://bvcoaching.in/2025/03/tour-of-thekkady-2025/, https://www.onmanorama.com/travel/kerala/2026/02/04/tour-of-thekkady-season-5-cycling-race.html
- Surface: tarmac/paved; organizer describes roads as generally well-maintained; no gravel/MTB sectors found. NOT a closed-road race — riders share open roads with traffic; a 2025 rider report notes buses/trucks and close calls in the dark early kilometers, plus a busier 7km Kattappana-Puliyanmala stretch. Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/about, https://www.tourofthekkady.com/faq, https://bvcoaching.in/2025/03/tour-of-thekkady-2025/
- Field size: ~240 total participants (race+tour) in 2026; 236 in 2025 (~100 in the race start). Source: https://www.onmanorama.com/travel/travel-news/2026/03/16/tour-of-thekkady-2026-winners.html, https://bvcoaching.in/2025/03/tour-of-thekkady-2025/
- Timing: electronic timing, mass start 5:30am (race)/5:45am (tour) on open roads. Categories: Men Elite 18+ (8hr cutoff), Men Masters 40+/Women 18+ (9hr cutoff), Tour 18+ (14hr cutoff). Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/termsandconditions
- Entry: paid registration, organizer-approval based; fee covers timing/certificate, medal, hydration, limited technical/medical support, bag transfer, finish food — not accommodation/food en route/merch/return transport. "Self-supported" refers to rider responsibility for repairs/food, not an unsupported ultra format — event still provides control-point hydration, medical/ambulance coverage, and bag transfer. Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com/termsandconditions, https://www.tourofthekkady.com/about
- History: launched by KEG Bikers, April 30 2022, ~54 riders in edition 1, growing to 236 (2025) and ~240 (2026); described by organizer as India's premier gran fondo, supported by Kerala Tourism. Source: https://www.tourofthekkady.com, https://bvcoaching.in/2025/03/tour-of-thekkady-2025/, https://www.onmanorama.com/travel/travel-news/2026/03/16/tour-of-thekkady-2026-winners.html

## Format verdict
Genuine mass-participation road gran fondo/cyclosportive paired with a non-competitive long-course "Tour" option. Not a travel product, virtual challenge, MTB/gravel event, or self-supported ultra. `discipline='gran_fondo'` is honest and unchanged.
