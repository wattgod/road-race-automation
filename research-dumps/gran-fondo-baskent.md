# Gran Fondo Başkent — Research Dump (Editorial Wave 8, 2026-07-24)

Research conducted via codex gpt-5.6-sol foreground web search, checked 2026-07-24.

## 1. Status and 2026 date

Active. Fifth edition scheduled for Sunday, 23 August 2026, start/finish at Bilkent Center, Ankara.
Confirmed by the Turkish Cycling Federation's 2026 Gran Fondo calendar
(https://bisiklet.org.tr/depolama/2026/03/2026-Granfondo-Takvimi.pdf) and July 2026 press coverage
(https://www.ekonomim.com/spor/ankara-visa-maximum-gran-fondo-baskente-besinci-kez-ev-sahipligi-yapacak-haberi-906725).

## 2. Course — 90.61 km / 1,237 m ascent (long course)

Current official long-course page (https://granfondobaskent.com/uzun-parkur/) and the completed 2025
race report (https://www.ekonomim.com/spor/gran-fondo-baskent-2025-ankaraya-deger-katti-haberi-839566)
both give 90.6 km / 1,237 m. No credible source supports the prior database figure of 99.8 km.

## 3. Course character

Government-filed 2025 route sheet (https://ankara.ktb.gov.tr/Eklenti/133803%2Cparkur-bilgilendirme-belgesipdf.pdf?0=)
identifies segments: Hoşdere Caddesi, Simon Bolivar Caddesi, Turan Güneş Bulvarı/Atakule, İncek Şehit
Savcı Mehmet Selim Kiraz Bulvarı, Turgut Özal Bulvarı, then Tulumtaş–Halaçlı–Koparan. Roughly 13.7 m
climbing per km — moderately hilly urban-opening-into-rolling-steppe, not a mountain-climbing course.
No named/categorized climbs beyond road segments.

## 4. Field, organizer, history

Organizer: 78 Event & Ajans; title sponsor Visa Maximum / İş Bankası Maximum Kart. First edition 2022,
making 2026 the fifth. Participation: 1,200 riders from 34 countries in 2024
(https://www.ankara.bel.tr/haberler/ankara-buyuksehirden-ucuncusu-duzenlenen-gran-fondo-baskent-yarisina-destek-17421),
nearly 1,000 in 2025 (https://www.dha.com.tr/spor/gran-fondo-baskent-2025-ankarada-gerceklestirildi-2705522).
No 2026 field cap published.

## 5. UCI / federation status

On the Türkiye Bisiklet Federasyonu 2026 Gran Fondo calendar as a traffic-closed amateur road race.
NOT a UCI Gran Fondo World Series qualifier — Başkent does not appear on the official UCI GFWS calendar
(https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/calendar/); Turkey's 2026 UCI qualifier is Granfondo Antalya
(6-8 November). Federation calendar inclusion should not be conflated with UCI-series affiliation.
