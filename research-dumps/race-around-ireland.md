# Race Around Ireland — editorial wave 9 research (2026-07-24)

Sources: codex/gpt-5.6-sol web search, foreground, 2026-07-24.

## Identity
Real, distinct ultra-endurance ROAD CYCLING race circumnavigating Ireland. Organized by
Alan Heary (owner/operator), racearoundireland.com. NOT the same event as Wicklow Sailing
Club's "Round Ireland Race" yacht race (roundireland.ie) — that is offshore sailing, unrelated
organization, different sport. The site itself does not explicitly address the name confusion,
but defines itself purely as a non-stop cycling event.
- https://racearoundireland.com/home
- https://alanheary.com/about/
- https://roundireland.ie/wp/index.php/about-the-race/ (the DIFFERENT sailing race)

## History / most recent completed edition
11th edition completed in 2019 (6th Irish Ultra Challenge). Course ~2,150-2,157.5km,
~22,000m climbing, anticlockwise loop from Trim, Co. Meath, past Newgrange, Causeway Coast,
Malin Head, Connemara, Cliffs of Moher, Ring of Kerry, Mizen Head, Wexford, Wicklow Mtns,
back to Trim (finish reported at Trim GAA in 2019).
- https://racearoundireland.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/RAI_-IUC_2019_Results.pdf
- https://ultracycling.com/calendar/race-around-ireland-2019/
- https://racearoundireland.com/germanys-bischoff-wins-race-around-ireland-2019

## Format
Single-stage, continuous clock, SUPPORTED (mandatory 3-person crew + vehicle, not
self-supported) road race on open public roads. Solo (M/F), tandem, 2/4/8-person relay teams.
21 time stations for check-ins, route book navigation (route not marked). 2019 time limits:
solo men 132h, solo women 144h, tandem/2-person 132h, 4/8-person teams 96h.
- https://racearoundireland.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Race_Around_Ireland_RuleBook_2019.pdf
- https://racearoundireland.com/faq
- https://racearoundireland.com/become-an-rai-volunteer

## Cancellations, no return since
- 2020: cancelled ("everything going on in the world"), organizer said hoped to return 2021.
- 2021: WUCA calendar confirms scheduled 23-28 Aug 2021 race cancelled.
- 2022: organizer's official cancellation statement: "We took the decision at the start of
  January to cancel this year's event." Cause: Omicron-era uncertainty/withdrawals. States
  "the 5-day race will come back in the future" but gives NO return year/date.
- 2023-2026: no announced edition found. Retrospective mentions only (Joe Barr Man of the Year
  profile 2023, Peter Ryan RTE profile 2024, Nicole Reist factsheet 2024) — all reference PAST
  completions, none announce a comeback. No indexed 2023-2026 comeback announcement on official
  FB/X handles (though those platforms partly block automated retrieval).
- A third-party aggregator (battistrada.com) lists a supposed 21 Aug 2022 edition — CONFLICTS
  with the organizer's own explicit 2022 cancellation notice; do not treat as evidence the race
  ran.

## Verdict for eligibility.status
Defensible present status: 11 completed editions through 2019; cancelled 2020/2021/2022;
organizer's last public statement (Jan 2022) expressed intent to return "in the future" with
no date; total silence 2023-2026 (4+ years). Chose "cancelled" over "defunct" because there is
a documented, if stale, organizer statement of intent to return and no explicit shutdown
statement (contrast with Birkebeinerrittet road pilot, where the organizer explicitly said the
concept didn't fit and discontinued it for good).
