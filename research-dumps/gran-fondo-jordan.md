# Research Dump: Gran Fondo Jordan (UCI Gran Fondo Jordan Dead Sea)

## Eligibility / status
- Status: **unknown** (held at prior value after sol adversarial review corrected an initial misread).
- Confirmed facts: a 3rd edition ran January 29, 2025, opening the 2025 UCI Gran Fondo World Series (the series' 15th season) — time trial Thursday, road-race stages Friday/Saturday at the Dead Sea (Movenpick hotel hub), Amman, Jordan. This matches the profile's own history text ("2025 edition marked the event's third year and the start of the UCI Gran Fondo World Series' 15th season") — **an initial pass in this research misdated this article as describing a January 2026 running; it actually describes January 2025.** Corrected after adversarial sol review flagged the date error.
- Source (curl 200): https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/PopUp/8348/uci-gran-fondo-jordan-dead-sea-opens-2025-uci-gran-fondo-world-series — states: "The 15th season of the UCI Gran Fondo World Series... will start this weekend with the UCI Gran Fondo Jordan Dead Sea," and that it "will be organized for the third time." No explicit calendar date in the article body; the "2025 season" framing and cross-reference to the 2025 UCI Gran Fondo World Championships (Oct 17-19, 2025) date this to January 2025, not 2026.
- A "Gran Fondo Jordan 2026" listing exists on the independent aggregator battistrada.com (https://battistrada.com/en/cycling-calendar/edition/gran-fondo-jordan-2026/46263/), dated January 28, 2026, part of the "UCI Gran Fondo World Series" circuit — but the same page also carries a **"Date cancelled"** annotation elsewhere on the page, and no results/finisher data for a January 2026 running were found on any source checked.
- Jordan does not appear on the UCI GFWS's own current forward-looking calendar page (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/calendar/, checked live July 2026).
- Official event site remains reachable (curl 200, JS-rendered, limited static content): https://www.ucigranfondojordan.com/ — states the event is "organised since 2023."
- **Net assessment:** genuinely ambiguous. Positive signal (aggregator lists a Jan 2026 edition as part of the UCI circuit) is undercut by a conflicting cancellation annotation on the same page and the absence of any confirmed 2026 results or a listing on UCI's own forward calendar. Held at "unknown" rather than upgraded to "active" — insufficient evidence either way.

## Claim: "UCI-sanctioned challenge" (final_verdict.should_you_race)
- **Verified true regardless of the above ambiguity.** Gran Fondo Jordan Dead Sea is/was an official UCI Gran Fondo World Series event and UCI World Championships qualifier — confirmed via UCI's own competition page, the official event site, and independent aggregator coverage for at least 3 confirmed past editions (2023 debut through the Jan 2025 3rd edition). The "UCI-sanctioned" framing describes the event's nature/heritage, not a claim about a specific upcoming date, so no correction to this claim is needed even with eligibility.status held at "unknown."
