## Course
Loop starting/finishing at Le Balcon de Villard/Côte 2000. Climbs: Col de la Croix Perrin (~4.2km @ 4.7%, km 15, summit 1,218m), through Gorges de la Bourne, Font d'Urle (~6km @ 5.5%, km 79, course high point ~1,500m), Col d'Herbouilly (~10km @ 6%, summit ~km 144), Côte 2000 finishing climb (~3km @ 5%, ~km 155). Fully paved, mostly open to normal traffic outside specified closure zones.

## Format check
Real, one-day, mass-participation paved-road gran fondo/cyclosportive with chip timing, corrals, category competition and a podium on the long course. Medium course gets a finish time but alphabetized-only (noncompetitive) results.
