# Fyn Rundt (Tour of Funen) — Editorial Wave 9 Research Notes

Source: codex exec (gpt-5.6-sol, web search), 2026-07-24.

## Identity
Fyen/Fyn Rundt is both a UCI Men Elite 1.2 one-day road race and, alongside it, a public
mass-participation sportive, run on the same day, same start/finish town. The public event
is chip-timed via Sportstiming but is not itself a UCI-sanctioned category — only the elite
race carries UCI 1.2 designation.
- Official elite info: https://fyenrundt.com/uci-elite-info-ny/
- Official public-ride info: https://fyenrundt.com/motion-info-ny/

## 2026 status
Confirmed held Sunday, May 3, 2026, start/finish in Middelfart.
- Official schedule: https://fyenrundt.com/uci-elite-info-ny/
- 2026 results: https://www.procyclingstats.com/race/albani-classic-fyen-rundt/2026/result/result/result

## Distances
2026 public sportive: 74, 113, and 177 km.
Elite race: 188 km competitive + ~10 km neutralized rollout (~198 km total, close to the
199.6 km figure previously on file — that number tracked the elite total, not any public
entry option).

## Field size
Elite 2026 archive: 150 starters, 119 finishers, 31 DNFs. No reliable published total found
for the public sportive field.

## Terrain
Rolling, not flat — elite 188 km route carries approximately 1,323 m of climbing per
ProCyclingStats course data, flat final kilometer.

## History
First held 1894, organized by Fyens Cykle Union. Public component added 1978. Elite and
public rides have shared the same event day since 2002.

## Name confusion check
"Fyn," "Fyen," "Funen," and "Tour of Funen" all refer to this same cycling race. Denmark
also has an unrelated sailing regatta "Fyn Rundt for Bevaringsværdige Sejlskibe"
(fyn-rundt.dk) — confirmed unrelated, no conflation risk for our profile. No running race
of the same name was found.
