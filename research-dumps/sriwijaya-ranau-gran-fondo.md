## Field
1,863 riders from 17 Indonesian provinces (2025, latest completed edition) — no
2026 field target published.

## Organizer/history
Jointly staged by South Sumatra provincial government and OKU Selatan regency as
sport-tourism promotion. First held November 9, 2019. 2025 billed as 7th edition.

## Criticisms
Ahead of 2025, officials themselves acknowledged need for clearer route marking,
better spectator control, stronger safety/medical coordination, and accelerated
road repairs. Event reportedly ran without major disruption despite these gaps.
