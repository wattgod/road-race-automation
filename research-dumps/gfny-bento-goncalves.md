# Research Dump: GFNY Bento Gonçalves

## Quick Facts
- Real GFNY World Series race in Bento Gonçalves, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil, in the Serra Gaúcha wine region
- Founded 2022 — GFNY's 2022 announcement called it a "new race" returning GFNY to Brazil; held October 16, 2022
- Ran in 2022, 2023, and (after a postponement) May 4, 2025 — three total editions, called the "third edition" in GFNY's 2025 coverage
- The planned October 2024 third edition was postponed following the catastrophic Rio Grande do Sul floods, ultimately held May 4, 2025 instead
- **Current status: absent from GFNY's live global calendar as of 2026-07-24.** GFNY's current race finder (through July 2027) does not list Bento Gonçalves or any Brazilian event. The official Bento homepage (bento.gfny.com) is reduced to "Subscribe to learn more" and still describes the 2025 course; the registration page has no date/widget/price; the results page links only 2022, 2023, and 2025 (no 2026 result)

## Course facts (corrected against real editions — vitals were stale/wrong)
The profile's prior 170.6 km / 2,500 m figures are unsupported by any documented edition. Real course history:
- **2022** (founding edition): announced 145 km/3,192 m; local post-event material reported ~143 km/2,963 m
- **2023** (second edition): recorded winning distance 135.9 km
- **2025** (third, last-held edition): official GFNY post-race report gives **122.5 km / 3,218 m** for the long course
- The abandoned/unverified regional 2026 plan advertised yet another configuration (124.5 km/2,367 m long, 73.9 km/1,484 m medium) — this describes a plan that appears to have collapsed, not a verified current course

**Route**: The original 2022-23 alignment included a 60 km western loop through the Vale dos Vinhedos wine region with historic cobbled sections, repeated 1-4 km climbs at 8-16% gradient, and a longest climb of 12 km at ~4%. The route was redesigned for 2025: Igreja Pipa, Caminhos de Pedra, Ponte de Ferro, Nova Roma do Sul, Casa Corba, Pinto Bandeira, and the Barracão climb back toward Alameda Fenavinho and Fundaparque — still Serra Gaúcha wine country, but not simply the old Vale dos Vinhedos loop. Prior profile content describing "Vale dos Vinhedos climbs" applies most directly to 2022-23, not the last-run 2025 course.

**Surface**: Road course, predominantly paved public roads, with historic cobbled sectors on the original 2022-23 route.

**Field size**: ~800 riders in both the 2022 and 2023 editions (historical). No current field/capacity published for any future edition.

**Entry fee**: Unknown/null — no current Bento-specific price published; the official help center confirms variable escalating pricing but nothing Bento-specific and current.

## Identity check
Confirmed: this is the real GFNY Bento Gonçalves road-cycling event in Bento Gonçalves, Rio Grande do Sul. GFNY's 2022 announcement names the event, host city, October 16 2022 date, Serra Gaúcha course, and bento.gfny.com official site. The 2025 post-race report identifies it as the third GFNY Bento Gonçalves edition. It is NOT GFNY Conservatória — a separate, distinct GFNY Brazil event in Rio de Janeiro state, with its own history (inaugural 2017, long hiatus, returned 2024). Bento Gonçalves was introduced as a new Brazilian race in 2022, not a rename of Conservatória; the two coexisted as distinct GFNY Brazil destinations, not duplicate profiles. Also unrelated to the Grande Prêmio Bento Gonçalves horse race or any other similarly-named local event.
