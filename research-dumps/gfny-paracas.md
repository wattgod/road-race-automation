## Course character
Mostly flat, fast paved racing through Paracas National Reserve, San Martín
port, roads near Pisco airport. Signature difficulty: persistent Pacific
coastal wind across exposed desert, not climbing (highest point only ~74m).
Scenery: desert, cliffs, ocean.

## History

June 2025. Runner-up for GFNY's 2024 "Best New Race" Athletes' Choice award
