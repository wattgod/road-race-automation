No decimal-precision distance published. Prior DB value of 209.2km had no solid source. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/course/ (2026 official course page)

## Course character corrections

- Osado Skyline is NOT part of the current 210km A course — it's a separate ~30km mountain road exceeding 900m; the mapped A-course circuit tops out at 136m. Source: https://www.visitsado.com/spot/detail0139/. This was a proposed error caught before publication.
- Gold-mining heritage at Aikawa (19km) is legitimate — official course map marks "Sado Gold Mines." Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/en/course/coursemap.pdf; https://sadotimes.sadokisen.co.jp/sado-color/sadolongride2026/.
- Shukunegi/coastal settlements: legitimate, described by Sado Tourism as a preserved Kitamae-bune merchant-shipping port settlement, not generic "fishing villages." Source: https://www.visitsado.com/en/sado/cycling/course1-1/.
- Noh theater culture is genuine island-wide cultural backdrop but not a documented on-course landmark — phrased as destination culture, not something riders necessarily see from the road. Source: https://www.visitsado.com/en/feature/nohstage/.
- Hardest hill sequence on course is specifically flagged by the organizer as arriving after Ogi (km 163+), late in the ride. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/en/course/.
- Numerous unlit tunnels require lights — 2026 organizer specifically recommended a high-visibility rear light. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/ (1 May 2026 lighting notice).

## Documented weaknesses/criticisms

- 2026 aid-station food shortage: organizer publicly apologized for delayed food delivery and stations running out. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/ (homepage notice).
- Island access logistics: Niigata-Ryotsu crossing ~2.5hr by car ferry or ~1hr7min by jetfoil (bicycle must be disassembled/bagged for jetfoil); Sado Kisen asked vehicle users to reserve in advance for 2026. Source: https://www.sadokisen.co.jp/news/20260416-2/ (16 Apr 2026); https://www.visitsado.com/en/sado/cycling/.
- Weather/access risk: organizer may cancel for bad weather/high waves/strong wind even if on-land conditions look fine; no refunds. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/guide/guide1_4.html; https://www.sado-longride.com/info/.
- Real cutoff structure: A1 starts 5:30am, A2 5:45am, both finish by 6:00pm; intermediate cutoffs Ryotsu 12:00, Tada 13:45, Ogi 15:00, Sobama 16:30. Source: https://www.sado-longride.com/en/course/; https://www.sado-longride.com/guide/guide2_10.html.
- Not fully closed-road: Sado City states roads remain open to ordinary traffic except limited sections. Source: https://www.city.sado.niigata.jp/uploaded/attachment/59731.pdf.
