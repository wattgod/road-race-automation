# Surrey Rumble — editorial wave 9 research (2026-07-24)

Sources: codex/gpt-5.6-sol web search, foreground, 2026-07-24.

## Identity
Confirmed Cobham, Surrey, ENGLAND (Surrey Hills) — not Surrey, British Columbia, Canada.
Organized by Twickenham Cycling Club, start/HQ at Downside Village Hall, routes through the
Surrey Hills.
- https://twickenhamcc.co.uk/events/surrey-rumble/
- https://www.britishcycling.org.uk/events/details/318031/Surrey-Rumble

## 2026 date correction
Confirmed Sunday 22 March 2026 (entries closed 17 March 2026), per both Twickenham CC and
British Cycling's official listing. The "17 May" date previously on file has no primary-source
support and is a third-party data error.

## Distance/elevation
Organizer lists 80mi/125km and 60mi/95km routes; British Cycling lists 129km/80mi and 97km/60mi.
Linked GPS route files measure ~77.7mi and ~58.2mi. 128.7km/80.0mi (already on file) is a
reasonable, internally-consistent long-route figure (80mi = 128.7km).
2,405m elevation is NOT supported by the organizer and is almost certainly excessive — published
route files for the Surrey Rumble long route cluster around 1,255-1,294m ascent (e.g. a 2022
route logged at 1,263m). Corrected to ~1,270m (GPS-dependent, organizer does not publish an
official figure).
- https://www.plotaroute.com/route/1838132 (2022 route, 1,263m)

## Status/history
2026 event ran as scheduled, no cancellation signal. First running 2008 (a contemporary 2009
report calls that year "the second running"). Apparent single pandemic-era gap; 2026 is roughly
the 18th edition (organizer does not officially number editions). 2027 British Cycling listing
already live, reporting 250+ entrants across both routes in 2026.
- https://roadcyclinguk.com/news/rides-news/the-surrey-rumble-cyclosportive.html
- https://www.britishcycling.org.uk/events/details/333871/Surrey-Rumble (2027 listing)

## Fees/field
British Cycling estimated ~200 long-route + ~100 short-route places (the "200" on file describes
one route, not total field). Fees: GBP22 before 31 Dec 2025, GBP26 after, GBP30 on the day if
available.
