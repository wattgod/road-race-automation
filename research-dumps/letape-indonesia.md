## Course character
Rolling southern-Lombok roads with short, steep climbs, remote villages,
rice paddies, beaches, coastal views. The 141 km route's hills overlook
Lombok's southern coastline.
https://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2020/02/27/mandalika-to-host-international-cycling-event-la-tour-de-france.html

## History
Announced 2020 (A.S.O. + local promoter Rocca); pandemic delays pushed the
first completed edition to 2022. Second edition 2023 (A.S.O. + local
promoter Veloism Danendra Aksata).

## Credible negatives
No sustained independent pattern of participant complaints found overall.
Documented concerns: damaged route sections and unresolved
security/road-preparation issues shortly before the 2022 debut; that race
recorded three crashes, including one rider hospitalized with a head
laceration.
