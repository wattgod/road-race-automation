## Course facts (organizer regulations vs route pages both cited — noted discrepancy)
| Route | Regs distance | Route-page distance | Elevation | Surface split |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Long | 118km | 110km | 2,740m | 55km white road (strade bianche) + 63km asphalt |
| Medium | 95km | 91km | 1,870m | 45km white road + 50km asphalt |
| Short | 73km | 73km | 1,258m | 43km white road + 30km asphalt |

Source: https://www.eroica.cc/regolamento-dolomiti/, https://www.eroica.cc/en/long-dolomites-route/, https://www.eroica.cc/en/percorso-medio-dolomiti/, https://eroica.cc/en/eroica-dolomiti/short-route-dolomiti

- Long route is ~47% unpaved (55/118km), medium ~47% (45/95km), short ~59% (43/73km). This is NOT a paved-roads-only event.
- Landmarks: short route via Cortina d'Ampezzo, Passo Tre Croci, Lake Misurina; medium adds white-road climb to Prato Piazza; long adds Prato Piazza + Cornetto di Confine/Markinkele toward the Austrian border. No organizer-published categorized-climb/gradient data.
- Equipment: mandatory vintage/steel bicycles — steel road-racing frames built through 1987 (narrow Alan/Vitus aluminium exception), or newer steel bikes built/equipped in vintage style: downtube shifters, toe clips/straps (no clipless pedals), external brake cables, low-profile wheels with >=32 spokes. MTBs explicitly excluded. Vintage/period clothing required. Source: https://www.eroica.cc/regolamento-dolomiti/
- Entry: individual online (MyEroica); 2026 prices EUR70 (club members) then EUR90/100/110 by registration window. Medical certificate required (competitive-cycling cert for 118km/95km; non-competitive cert accepted for 73km). Source: https://www.eroica.cc/en/dolomites-registrations/, https://www.eroica.cc/regolamento-dolomiti/
- Field: 500-bib regulatory cap; ~400 participated in 2025. Source: https://www.eroica.cc/en/dolomites-regulations/, https://www.osttirol-heute.at/wirtschaft/innichen-rueckblick-auf-einen-tollen-sommer-2025/
- Cutoff: common 08:00 start; long-route signage/food-stop support begins being removed from Dobbiaco at 17:00 — ~9 hours of supported course time, NOT the 13 hours previously in our profile. Source: https://www.eroica.cc/regolamento-dolomiti/
- History: launched 2017 (San Candido edition) — a distinct event from the original L'Eroica in Tuscany/Siena (1997); do not inherit the Tuscany founding date. Source: https://www.sportfair.it/ciclismo/ciclismo-il-maltempo-non-ha-fermato-a-san-candido-la-prima-edizione-di-eroica-dolomiti/604772/, https://www.suedtirolerland.it/en/leisure-activities/mountain-biking-and-cycling/top-events/eroica-dolomiti/

## Format verdict
Non-competitive vintage-bike heritage ride (ciclostorica) — organizer explicitly calls it non-competitive; open roads, normal traffic rules, completion via checkpoint stamp book, no ranked results. Not a normal competitive road sportive, not MTB (MTBs banned), not a modern gravel race despite substantial white-road content. Best framing: supported, mixed-surface, vintage-equipment-mandatory heritage ride and festival. `discipline='sportive'` is misleading without a taxonomy caveat given the majority-unpaved surface and non-competitive/vintage-equipment format — flagged via catalog_flags per the flandrien-ride.json precedent rather than changed.
