# Research Dump: Atlas Gran Fondo

## Quick Facts
- Official event page: https://www.atlasgranfondo.com/granfondo/ — active, confirmed 2026 edition.
- 2026 dates: Friday Nov 20 (village/packet pickup), Saturday Nov 21 (gravel/MTB, 120/80 km), **Sunday Nov 22 (road gran fondo, 160/120/80/40 km, 08:30 start)**. Prior database date "November 21" mismatched the road-race day — that's the gravel/MTB day.
- Location: Taroudant, Morocco (Avenue Moulay Ismail event village). Confirmed correct.
- Organizer: Association Multi Social Challenge; site publisher of record is SARL Morocco All Races.
- Founding year: not verifiable as an "inaugural" year from authoritative sourcing — earliest substantiated edition is 2025. Keep `founded: null` rather than fabricate a year.

## Course Profile (2026 road gran fondo)
- **160 km / ~2,100 m D+** (headline long option) — organizer-validated via Finishers listing and Active registration category; the previously stored 159.3 km / 1,800 m pair is not defensible against current sourcing.
- Other road distances: 120 km (~1,500 m D+), 80 km (~790 m D+, main climbing section ~km 27-46 reaching ~940 m altitude), 40 km (elevation not published).
- Route: Taroudant start/finish loops; no current public GPX or named-climb sheet exposed (official "OpenRunner" link redirects to OpenRunner's generic homepage; course-page map image is still labeled `map-gran-fondo-2025`).
- Surface: UCI-compliant road bicycles only on open public roads; e-bikes prohibited. Distinct from the Saturday gravel/MTB courses.
- Field size: not published anywhere (official program, registration, Active listing) — keep as unknown/null, not "N/A".
- 2026 international entry fees: 160 km €95 / 120 km €85 / 80 km €80 / 40 km €55. Moroccan-resident pricing not visible on the public resident form.
- Eligibility rules: minimum age 18, valid medical certificate or cycling licence, approved helmet, roadworthy bike; e-bikes banned.

## Identity check
Confirmed real, physical, organized event — bib pickup, timing, feed zones, medical support, road-bike-only rules. Not a motorcycle event, not virtual/Zwift. The previously stored YouTube entry ("BMW GS 1200 ADV MOROCCO DESERT 2015") is unrelated dirt-bike touring footage misfiled under this profile — confirmed wrong and purged. Not to be confused with the Atlas Mountain Race (a separate, unsupported ~1,200 km Moroccan gravel ultra with no relation to this event).

## Naming/rebrand notes
- 2025 regional calendars referred to the event as "Atlas Gran Fondo Pikala" (Pikala likely a sponsor/partner suffix, not the canonical name) with different reported distances (150/110 km) — treat as historical naming variance, not a separate event.
- The site's legal notice contains leftover boilerplate referencing "L'EPIC DESERT RACE," suggesting template reuse across the operator's other events — lowers confidence in unattributed site copy but does not invalidate the event itself.
- Some syndicated calendar listings reverse the road/gravel day order in descriptive text while reproducing the correct schedule elsewhere on the same page; official homepage and program consistently place gravel/MTB on Nov 21 and road on Nov 22.
