## UCI Affiliation — verified
This is a genuine UCI Gran Fondo World Series event, not borrowed/marketing-only UCI branding. It is an early qualifier for the **2027 UCI Gran Fondo World Championships in France** (not the already-completed 2026 Worlds). Men 19-59 and women 19-49 qualify over the 132 km course; men 60+ and women 50+ qualify over the 71 km course. Open-category riders do NOT qualify.

## Course
132 km loop starts/finishes at La Nucía's Camilo Cano Olympic Stadium, heading inland through Finestrat and Relleu before climbing into the Sierra de Aitana toward the Safari Aitana area (just above 1,000 m — NOT the true 1,558 m Aitana summit, despite organizer marketing calling it "Alto de Aitana"). A long middle section links Gorga, Fageca, Famorca, and Castell de Castells over rolling climbs. Decisive late feature: timed Coll de Rates ascent at km 99-105.3 (6.6 km at 5.4% average), then a technical descent and rolling return through Tàrbena, Bolulla, Callosa, and Polop.
