## Course character
Alto Minho terrain: green Atlantic valleys, an inland mountain section, fast
coastal return. Skirts Serra d'Arga through Amonde, Meixedo, Lanheses, climbs
Cabracao (3km at 6.6%), extends toward Paredes de Coura via Agualonga, Rubiaes,
Sapardos, Covas; principal late difficulty is the climb to Sopos (4.5km at
6.2%) before Vilar de Mouros, Venade, Caminha, and the return to Viana.
Rolling-to-mountainous rather than alpine. Open roads, Highway Code in force
(not fully closed).
Source: https://bikeservice.pt/event/viana-granfondo/

## Organizer & history
Launched 19 March 2023 by Bikeservice as part of Viana do Castelo's European
City of Sport year, with 131/84/62km routes and ~1,700-2,000 riders in its
debut. Fourth edition in 2026; now the opening round of Bikeservice's
Superprestigio amateur series.
Source: https://www.altominho.tv/site/2023/03/10/primeira-edicao-do-viana-granfondo-realiza-se-a-19-de-marco/
