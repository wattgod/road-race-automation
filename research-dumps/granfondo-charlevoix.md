## Course / history
Course borrows sections of the Grand Prix's elite road-race course for 2026,
covered at cyclosportive (non-competitive, timed) pace. Named tough sectors:
Saint-Irénée climb (short, steep) and Rang Saint-Laurent (rolling, windy,
frequently rough/granular pavement per regional coverage).
https://numerique.banq.qc.ca/patrimoine/details/52327/4654549

Organizer roots: first Baie-Saint-Paul Quebec Cup in 1998, Club Cycliste de
Charlevoix incorporated December 1999, rebranded Vélo Charlevoix in 2022.
FQSC's "28th edition" (2026) numbering tracks the broader Grand Rendez-Vous
Cycliste de Charlevoix program, not proof the Granfondo itself dates to
1999 — no founding year asserted in the profile.
https://www.velocharlevoix.ca/a-propos/

## Criticism
No organizer-specific scandal found. Documented drawbacks: rough/granular
