**Two sub-issues found:**
1. **Nation count is stale.** Tour-Magazin's 2026 preview (published close to the event) states: "Am 28. Juni 2026 werden rund 3000 Teilnehmer aus über 40 Nationen erwartet" (~3,000 participants from over 40 nations expected). The file's "30 nations" figure is outdated. **CORRECTED in JSON to "40+ nations."**
2. **"One of Europe's largest cycling marathons" is an overreach given the actual field size.** 3,000 total riders (1,500 per route, hard-capped) is a *sold-out, in-demand* field for a niche tri-border Alpine event, but it is not large by the standard of Europe's biggest gran fondos for comparison: L'Étape du Tour (~16,000), Maratona dles Dolomites (~9,000), Ötztaler Radmarathon (~4,000). No source found that ranks Dreiländergiro among Europe's largest events by field size — the closest defensible superlative is that it sells out within hours/days due to its 1,500-per-route cap and cult following in DACH cycling culture. **CORRECTED in JSON: replaced "one of Europe's largest cycling marathons" with "one of the Alps' most in-demand cycling marathons," which is supported (hard cap, sells out fast, per AGB + Tour-Magazin).**
