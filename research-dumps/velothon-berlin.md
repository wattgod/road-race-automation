# Research Dump: Velothon Berlin / VeloCity Berlin

## Quick Facts
- **Current status (2026-07-24): officially PAUSED for 2025 and 2026, not permanently discontinued.** SCC EVENTS' own homepage says it is "suspending the event for 2025 and 2026" while evaluating the unusually high costs of security and road closures, with a stated goal of finding a sustainable continuation model. Berlin's official events portal independently says "Will not take place in 2026" (updated 20 January 2026). No organizer announcement of a 2027 return, and no announcement of permanent closure either — this is a genuine pause, not a "defunct" event.
- Last held: **Sunday, August 4, 2024** — NOT July 28, 2024. July 28, 2024 was the registration deadline, which the previously-filed profile appears to have mistaken for the event date.
- 2024 course: **100 km / 62.1 mi long course, 60 km / 37.3 mi short course** — NOT 159.3 km/99 mi as previously filed. Start/finish on Straße des 17. Juni between the Victory Column and Brandenburg Gate. Long-course minimum speed 25 km/h, time limit 3:45.
- Elevation: no organizer-published numeric figure could be found for the 2024 (or any recent) edition — the previously-filed 610 m/2,000 ft has no traceable source and should be nulled out, not presented as verified.
- Route: south through Kleinmachnow, Stahnsdorf, Ahrensdorf and Großbeeren before returning to Berlin; organizer describes the Brandenburg-area section as very flat.

## Sponsor/Organizer Chronology (dedupe-critical, corrects a wrong working assumption)
The correct chronology is NOT simply "Velothon Berlin -> ProRace Berlin -> VeloCity Berlin." ProRace Berlin was a separate professional race ADDED ALONGSIDE an already-existing mass-participation Velothon, not a former name for the whole amateur event:

| Period | Identity |
|---|---|
| 2008-2012 | Mass-participation event begins as Škoda Velothon Berlin; SCC EVENTS provided partial organization 2008-2018 |
| 2011-2013 | Separate UCI 1.1 professional race "ProRace Berlin" added alongside the existing amateur Velothon (~180 km pro race attached to the 4th Velothon) |
| 2013-2015 | Garmin replaces Škoda as mass-event title sponsor (3-year deal); professional component becomes "Garmin ProRace Berlin" (2013) then "Garmin Velothon Berlin" (2014-2015) |
| After 2015 | The recurring UCI 1.1 professional race ends after 5 editions; the mass-participation Velothon continues separately |
| 2017-2018 | Amateur event runs as "EuroEyes Velothon Berlin" with 60/120/180 km options |
| 2019 | Velothon Berlin CANCELLED after Ironman (who held the event rights/license) and SCC EVENTS (the local delivery partner) failed to agree on licensing |
| Planned 2020 | SCC announces its own successor, "VeloCity Berlin," rather than continuing under Ironman's Velothon license |
| 2020-2021 | Planned VeloCity launch postponed twice for the pandemic |
| 2022 | "VeloCity – The Power of Berlin" holds its inaugural edition, July 3, 2022 |
| 2023-2024 | Generali becomes title sponsor ("GENERALI VeloCity Berlin"); Generali's own announcement confirms SCC launched VeloCity in 2022 |
| 2025-2026 | Official two-year pause for cost/sustainability evaluation |

Correct founding year for the mass-participation continuity lineage: **2008** (Škoda Velothon Berlin), not 2011. VeloCity is best understood as SCC's successor/relaunch after the Ironman licensing split — not merely a sponsor-name change on the same product.

## Identity Check
Velothon was a multi-city UCI-affiliated series: the UCI's 2016 "Velothon Majors" announcement explicitly listed Cardiff/Wales, Berlin, and Stockholm as three SEPARATE city events, each with its own date and status:
- Velothon Wales (Cardiff/South Wales): final mass-participation edition 2018, organizers said no plans for 2019+
- Velothon Stockholm: launched 2015, ran through 2017, closed due to insufficient participation
- This database's corpus was checked: only ONE standalone Velothon profile exists (`velothon-berlin.json`, this one). Wales appears only as prose mentioned in another profile's alternatives field, not as its own race file; Stockholm has no standalone profile at all. No dedupe/merge action needed — this profile should describe ONLY the Berlin lineage and not be confused with or absorb Wales/Stockholm history.

## Dedupe determination
No duplicate profile exists for Velothon Berlin/VeloCity Berlin under a different name in the 425-race corpus. This profile is the sole and correct record for the Berlin lineage. Recommend keeping the display name reflecting the current organizer branding (VeloCity Berlin) while retaining "Velothon Berlin" historical context and the slug as-is.
