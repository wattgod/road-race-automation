# Race Around The Netherlands — editorial wave 9 research (2026-07-24)

Sources: codex/gpt-5.6-sol web search, foreground, 2026-07-24.

## Identity / organizer
Real, recurring Dutch ultra-cycling event with results published 2018-2026. Organized by
Germany-based Adventure Bike Racing / Michael Wacker — NOT "De Proloog" (De Proloog is the host
venue in Amerongen) and not an org called "Ultracycling NL." Not related to RAAM, LEL, or Dutch
pro road races — the mismatched youtube_data currently on file (Cape Town wind cancellation,
Dutch national championship road race footage, generic Dutch touring videos) is unrelated
content that should be removed.
- https://www.adventurebikeracing.com/ratn/ (canonical official page)
- https://www.adventurebikeracing.com/imprint/
- https://deproloog.cc/evenement/race-around-the-netherlands-2026/ (venue listing, redirects to organizer)

## Format/route
Fixed anticlockwise loop, start/finish De Proloog, Amerongen. 2026 manual: ~1,950km / ~7,000m
elevation, 99% paved. North Loop variant option ~1,315km/~800m. Fully self-supported/unsupported
race: outside material assistance and drafting prohibited; ordinary public services (shops,
hotels) permitted. Solo + teams up to 4.
- https://www.adventurebikeracing.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/RATN2026-manual.pdf

## 2026 edition — happened
Started 2 May 2026, 08:00, from De Proloog; finish cutoff 10 May 18:00 = 202 hours (8d10h),
consistent with the "8 days" cutoff already on file. Winner (first full-loop solo finisher):
Tiemen Van Tuinen, 3d18h10m. Field size far exceeds the "20-50" currently on file — official
results list well over 50 entrants.
- https://www.adventurebikeracing.com/ratn/
