# Granfondo Bratislava — Research Dump (Editorial Wave 8, 2026-07-24)

Research conducted via codex gpt-5.6-sol foreground web search, checked 2026-07-24.

## 1. 2026 status — cancelled for 2026, not permanently discontinued

Organizer announced on 17 February 2026 that the planned fifth adult edition "will not take place"
after title sponsor NAY withdrew. Described explicitly as a pause; organizer states hope to revive in
2027. Planned date was 2 August 2026. The separate Junior Fondo still runs 13 September 2026.
Source: https://granfondobratislava.sk/v-roku-2026-si-davame-pauzu/,
https://granfondobratislava.sk/detske-preteky/propozicie-junior-fondo-2026/

## 2. Course — 132 km / 1,286 m (2025, last completed edition)

Official 2025 regulations: 132 km Gran Fondo / 1,286 m, plus 70 km / 294 m Medio Fondo.
Pezinská Baba is real and race-specific — the long route crossed it twice, confirmed independently by
the City of Pezinok's own route notice. Route genuinely traverses the Malé Karpaty (Little Carpathians),
including Pezinská Baba, Modra and Červený Kameň.
Source: https://granfondobratislava.sk/propozicie/,
https://www.pezinok.sk/novinka/9302/peloton-cyklistickych-pretekov-nay-gran-fondo-bratislava-opat-prejde-cez-pezinsku-babu

## 3. History — four editions, founded 2022, not 2016

Adult editions ran 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025. Organizer's 2022 race book describes participants as being
present at the "birth" of a new race — founded_year 2016 is unsupported. No UCI Gran Fondo World Series
affiliation found; event does not appear on the UCI GFWS calendar
(https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/calendar/) and organizer materials only say it is "inspired by"
the international gran fondo format. Appears domestically on the Slovak federation's Merida Road
Cup/Cycling for All calendar.

## 4. Field, organizer, prestige

Organizer: CK D3-RACE TEAM Bratislava; 2025 co-organizers ELCOP Event Agency, VeloCity, Happy Kids;
race director Norbert Kirschner. Reported ~500 adult riders across the two 2025 pelotons plus 150
children; 2024 also near 500 adults, ~380 in the 2022 inaugural year. Supports prestige 2/5: credible,
well-produced regional Slovak event with some international participation, not a major Central European
destination fondo or UCI qualifier.
Source: https://granfondobratislava.sk/nay-gran-fondo-bratislava-2025-je-za-nami/,
https://granfondobratislava.sk/dakujeme-ucastnikom-nay-gran-fondo-bratislava-2024-za-skvelu-sportovu-atmosferu/
