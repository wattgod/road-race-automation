# Gran Fondo Curacaví — Editorial Wave 10 Research Notes

Source: codex exec (gpt-5.6-sol, web search), 2026-07-24.

## Eligibility
**Status: active.** Official site (curl-verified 200) announces a sixth edition for
18 April 2027, following the completed fifth edition on 19 April 2026. Registration button
currently points to the finalized 2026 Welcu listing
(https://welcu.com/epic-rides-chile-spa/granfondocuracavi2026); 2027 registration not yet
live.

## Vitals
Next edition advertised as 130km on 18 April 2027. Latest detailed official course spec
(2026, curl-verified PDF): 127km, ~1,200m climbing, 700m max altitude, starting/finishing
at Fundo El Molino, Curacaví.

**Location correction**: Curacaví is in Melipilla Province, Santiago Metropolitan Region —
NOT Valparaíso Region as this profile previously stated. The course crosses into
Casablanca, Valparaíso Region, before returning through María Pinto (which is how the
Valparaíso association likely originated).

## Course character
Paved, rolling-to-hilly wine-country loop: 16km neutralized opening + 111km competitive
section. Signature ascent: El Pangue, reaching the course high point at 700m, followed by
a fast, technical descent into Casablanca. Cuesta Ibacache is the other named
climbing/descending test, followed by a faster run through Chorombo toward Curacaví.

## History
Debuted 24 April 2022, organized by Fernando "Feña" Almuna and Epic Rides Chile — founding
year corrected to 2022 (not 2021 as previously on file). Reached its fifth edition in
2026. Epic Rides Chile also produces Gran Fondo Nevados de Chillán.

## Criticism
Safety is the clearest concern: roads stay open to normal traffic, riders must be
mechanically/nutritionally self-sufficient, and a participant suffered a serious
high-speed crash descending El Pangue in the 2026 edition. A 2022 rider report had already
flagged dirt, potholes and steep curves on that same descent plus deceptive curves on the
Ibacache descent (historical observations, not verified current conditions). Rules provide
no refund for voluntary withdrawal and only limited remedies for organizer cancellation.
