# Research Dump: L'Étape Ireland

## Quick Facts
- Location: Carrick-on-Suir, County Tipperary, Ireland — hometown of Sean Kelly and Sam Bennett.
- Status: genuinely part of ASO's official L'Étape by Tour de France series (appears on ASO's own 2026 calendar), not a Tour-themed imitation. Locally produced by Kerry-based Elite Events Management (race director Oliver Kirwan).
- Founded 2024 (first edition); 2025 was the second edition, held September 28, 2025.
- 2026 edition: confirmed in principle for Carrick-on-Suir but date listed as "TBD" on ASO's official 2026 L'Étape series calendar as of research date. A third-party listing claiming September 28, 2026 conflicts with ASO's TBD status (that date is also a Monday) and should not be treated as confirmed.
- 2025 routes: Long 150 km / 1,932 m; Short 94 km / 1,314 m (marketed loosely as "100K" despite being 94 km). Both start/finish in Carrick-on-Suir. ~80% closed roads on the long route.
- Elevation data inconsistency: one official guide gives 1,737 m for the long route vs. 1,932 m on the detailed route-release page — official copy is not internally consistent; treat any single figure as approximate/edition-specific.
- Field size: ~1,400 actual starters in 2025 per ASO's post-event recap; nearly 2,000 had registered by Sept 4, 2025, implying a meaningful no-show rate. No 2026 number or cap published.
- Timed climbs: Ticincor Hill (4.9 km at 7.9%) and Seskin Hill (1.5 km at 7.9%), plus a timed sprint near Lemybrien.
- Rider feedback (2025): broadly positive on route, marshalling, atmosphere, and feed stops. Complaints centered on high entry fees/value and scarce, expensive Carrick-on-Suir accommodation during event week.
- Separate, unrelated organizational issue: in 2024, a different L'Étape Ireland edition location (Killarney) drew councillor/trader criticism over late communication about road closures — not the same host town as the current Carrick-on-Suir edition, but worth noting as a pattern of local-logistics friction in the series' early years.
- Official registration platform (2025) was Eventmaster; that listing is now closed. No live 2026 registration page as of research date.
