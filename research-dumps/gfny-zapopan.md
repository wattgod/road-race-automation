# Research Dump: GFNY Zapopan

## Quick Facts
- Real GFNY World Series race in Zapopan/Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico, part of the GFNY Global Endurance Sports Series
- Founded 2022 — GFNY announced the inaugural race for October 2, 2022, initially marketed "Ford GFNY Zapopan–Guadalajara" (Ford as title sponsor)
- Ran 2022, 2023, and 2025 (no recorded 2024 edition — results jump from 2023 straight to 2025; the municipality calls 2025 the "third edition")
- **Current status: `unknown`, not `active`.** No official 2026 edition or date can be verified as of 2026-07-24:
  - GFNY's current race finder (through announced 2027 events) does not list Zapopan
  - The official Zapopan homepage still shows the 2025 course and "2025 Champions"
  - The official registration iframe identifies the event as "GFNY Zapopan 2025, May 4, 2025" and says no tickets are available
  - The official results page has 2025, 2023, and 2022 only — no 2026 result
  - A third-party listing (Battistrada) shows a May 3, 2026 date but now describes it in the past tense and asks users to submit a future edition — not corroborated by GFNY, and the prior on-file eligibility note claiming the official site listed a 2026 date is incorrect
  - No official cancellation/closure announcement found — `cancelled`/`defunct` would both overstate the evidence; `unknown` is the accurate call

## Course facts (last verified edition: 2025)
The profile's prior 146.4 km / 1,234 m is wrong — those numbers come from the original PRE-RACE 2022 announcement, not what was actually run. The actual inaugural 2022 race was subsequently reported by GFNY as 129.3 km with 875 m elevation gain. The course has changed nearly every year:
- 2022 (actual, per GFNY recap): 129.3 km / 875 m
- 2023: 126 km long / 63 km short
- 2025 (last held): 125 km long / 62 km medium (post-race municipal reporting; pre-race publicity had rounded the long route to 130 km)

**Elevation**: not published for the 2025 route specifically — do not retain 1,234 m as a current figure. The official course page gives an operating altitude of 1,680 m above sea level (elevation of the location, not elevation GAIN), and states maximum inclines were only ~3%.

**Route**: Start/finish at Parque de las Niñas y los Niños. Uses Laureles, Ávila Camacho, Glorieta de La Normal, Circunvalación Álvarez del Castillo, López Mateos, Camino al ITESO, Periférico, and Guadalupe; long-course riders complete a second lap.

**Surface**: Paved urban roads/avenues — a fully road-based metropolitan course.

**Key climbs**: None named. GFNY characterizes the course as fast and peloton-oriented with maximum grades of only ~3% — altitude (1,680m) rather than sustained climbing is the course's principal physiological complication.

**Field size**: 2,074 registered riders actually participated in 2025 (the pre-race "more than 3,000 expected" figure was a forecast, not the actual turnout).

**Entry fee**: No current/2026 fee — the stale official registration iframe says no tickets are available; it displays a former US$350 + US$15.50 processing fee, which should not be treated as a current 2026 price.

## Identity check
Confirmed: this is the real GFNY Zapopan road-cycling gran fondo in Zapopan and Guadalajara, Jalisco — not a motorcycle event, not a different Guadalajara-area race. GFNY's announcement places the event in Zapopan, part of the GFNY Global Endurance Sports Series, with a competitive cycling course. Official rules define it as an individually timed bicycle ride with competitive long-course and non-competitive medium-course classifications. Municipal government reporting independently confirms it as a cycling event for professional/amateur riders traversing both Zapopan and Guadalajara.

## Name and sponsor history
The inaugural 2022 and 2023 editions were marketed as "Ford GFNY Zapopan–Guadalajara" / "Ford GFNY Zapopan" (Ford as title sponsor; "Zapopan–Guadalajara" describing the host metro area). By 2025 the public name had simplified to "GFNY Zapopan" — the municipal contract calls it "Gran Fondo New York Zapopan, also known as GFNY Zapopan." This is one continuous event lineage, not separate races — do not create a duplicate "GFNY Guadalajara" entry. The official results archive jumping from 2023 straight to 2025 confirms no recorded 2024 edition (not a data gap — the municipality itself calls 2025 the third edition, meaning 2024 was skipped).
