## Course character
Ten flat coastal km precede relentless, scenic Marche hill-country rollers —
no single alpine centerpiece. Difficulty from repeated short climbs, descents,
late-race ramps through Macerata, Filottrano, Recanati.

## Signature climbs
Porto Potenza Picena (6.5km/4.5%), rollers to 8%, Montecassiano/Montefano
pitches ~5-7%, Filottrano's stepped 6-7% climb finishing 8-9%, ~2km at 6-7%
near Montefiore, late 2.5km at 6% before descent to Porto Recanati.

## Organizer/history
ASD Bike Division, led by former pro Andrea Tonti; launched 2021 as Gran Fondo
Vincenzo Nibali-5Mila Marche to promote Marche cycling tourism. 2026 = 6th edition.

## Criticisms
Official documents conflict on medium-route distance (see above). Force-majeure
cancellation policy: no cash refund, only following-year transfer minus EUR10.
