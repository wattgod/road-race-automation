## Identity check

Confirmed. Gran Fondo Kazakhstan is a real mass-participation road event in
Almaty. The organizer lists separate 95 km Gran Fondo and 50 km Media Fondo
road-bike categories, plus a distinct 37 km MTB category. The road-event
identity is sound; the MTB option does not make the Gran Fondo itself a
gravel/MTB race.

Important naming caveat: the organizer markets the event as the "Gran Fondo
World Championship," but its regulations identify the title as a championship
"according to the GFWT version." This is Gran Fondo World Tour (GFWT)
branding, not the UCI Gran Fondo World Championships. Neither the organizer
site nor the Kazakhstan Cycling Federation page evidences UCI sanction for
this specific race — it should be read and marketed as GFWT-network
branding, explicitly NOT a UCI World Championship.

The file's `founded: 2022` is wrong. An official organizer interview says the
first amateur race was held in late September 2016.
https://www.granfondo.com.kz/ru/news/4/
