## Pro Tour of Oman history 2020-2026 (for context only — NOT this profile's subject)
- 2020: scheduled 11-16 Feb; cancelled 22 Jan for national mourning (death of Sultan Qaboos),
  not COVID. https://www.cyclingnews.com/news/aso-confirms-cancellation-of-2020-tour-of-oman/
- 2021: postponed 1 Feb (COVID), never rescheduled.
- 2022: held 10-15 Feb, 6 stages/888km.
- 2023: held 11-15 Feb; inaugural Muscat Classic 10 Feb.
- 2024: held 10-14 Feb; storms shortened stages 3-5.
- 2025: held 8-12 Feb; Adam Yates won overall.
- 2026: held 7-11 Feb; Christian Scaroni won overall.
