# Research Dump: Gran Fondo Asheville

## Eligibility / status
- Status: **active**. Gran Fondo Asheville is listed on the official 2026 Gran Fondo National Series calendar, run July 19, 2026, at Asheville Yards (75 Coxe Ave), presented by the Applewood Manor Foundation.
- Source (curl 200): https://www.granfondonationalseries.com/gfns-events-2026 — official 2026 series calendar lists 7 events: Gran Fondo Florida (Dade City, FL, Mar 22), Tour of Georgia Gran Fondo (Helen, GA, Apr 19), Highlands Gran Fondo (Butler, NJ, Jun 7), Gran Fondo Utah (Payson, UT, Jun 13), **Gran Fondo Asheville (Asheville, NC, Jul 19)**, Boone Gran Fondo (Boone, NC, Aug 2), Gran Fondo Maryland & National Championships (Frederick, MD, Sep 20).
- Official event page (curl 200): https://www.granfondonationalseries.com/gran-fondo-asheville/

## Claim: "Part of Gran Fondo National Series (America's original series)"
- **Verified true for 2026.** Gran Fondo Asheville is one of 7 confirmed stops on the 2026 Gran Fondo National Series (owned/operated by Suarez / SENA Gran Fondo National Series). Confirmed on both the series' own 2026 events page and independent aggregator granfondoguide.com.
- Secondary source: https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/GranFondoNationalSeries (2026 SENA Gran Fondo National Series dashboard, lists Asheville among series events).

## Claim: "Hosted 2022 USA Cycling Gran Fondo National Championships"
- **Verified true.** The 2022 USA Cycling Gran Fondo National Championships were held during Gran Fondo Asheville p/b Applewood Manor on July 17, 2022, starting/finishing at Rabbit Rabbit in downtown Asheville. Riders qualified via Gran Fondo National Series results and competed for age-group national titles across four timed segments on the 100-mile Gran route.
- Official USA Cycling source (curl 200): https://usacycling.org/article/asheville-north-carolina-announced-as-host-city-of-2022-usa-cycling-gran-fondo-national-championships-presented-by-applewood-manor
- Corroborating: https://www.sportstravelmagazine.com/gran-fondo-national-series-returns-to-asheville-in-2022/ ; https://www.granfondonationalseries.com/blog/2022/6/30/gran-fondo-asheville-keeps-national-championship-cycling-in-north-carolina-for-second-year

## Claim: 2026 event date "July 19/20"
- **Verified: single-day event, July 19, 2026 (Sunday).** No evidence of a two-day (19th AND 20th) format for the 2026 edition — official pages consistently show Sunday, July 19, 2026 as the race day. "July 19/20" in the profile likely reflects a Saturday packet-pickup (19th) / Sunday race (20th)-style weekend framing seen in other GFNS events, but the race itself is a single Sunday event on July 19. Not corrected in this pass since the vitals field just says "July" (no specific day claimed there) — flagging for future accuracy pass if a specific "July 19-20" date string appears elsewhere in the profile.
- Corroborating: https://ashevilleonbikes.com/events/gran-fondo-asheville ("2026 Gran Fondo Asheville returns Sunday, July 19!")

## 2027 note (context, not a correction)
- Separate from the above: Asheville has also been announced as host city for the **2027** USA Cycling Gran Fondo National Championships (per the eligibility source already on file: granfondonationalseries.com/blog/2026/6/22/asheville-north-carolina-announced-as-host-city-of-2027-usa-cycling-gran-fondo-national-championships-presented-by-applewood-manor-foundation). This is a forward-looking designation, not a rewrite of the 2022 history — both are true and non-conflicting (host in 2022, host again in 2027).
