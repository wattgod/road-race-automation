## Claim: "UCI Gran Fondo Hangzhou Qiandao Lake" / UCI affiliation (display_name, tagline)
- **Verified true.** This is an official stop on the UCI Gran Fondo World Series calendar, run under UCI sanctioning, with a time trial (21 km, Liangzhou city) and a mass-start gran fondo (97 km, north side of Qiandao Lake). Confirmed on the UCI Gran Fondo World Series' own official pages (ucigranfondoworldseries.com) and the dedicated official China event site (ucigranfondochina.com).

## Claim: "China's first UCI Gran Fondo World Series" (tagline)
- **Verified true.** UCI Gran Fondo Hangzhou Qiandao Lake (and Xiang Lake) was the first-ever UCI Gran Fondo in China, debuting September 6-7, 2025. A later Chinese UCI GFWS stop, Gran Fondo Hainan, ran December 12-14, 2025 — after Hangzhou's debut, so the "first in China" claim holds chronologically even though both now appear on the same 2025 UCI GFWS China calendar.
- Source: https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/qiandao-lake-ready-for-record-breaking-second-edition-of-uci-gran-fondo-china/ (explicitly states the 2025 debut was "the first ever UCI Gran Fondo in China").
