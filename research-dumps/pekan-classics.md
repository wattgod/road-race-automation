## Distance/elevation correction
Official UCI World Series pre-race report (19 June 2025) gives:
- Gran Fondo: 162.7km / 1,072m (not 762m as previously listed)
- Medio Fondo: 93.5km / 470m
The old "762m/2500ft" figure is not supported by official material and should be corrected to
~1,072m / ~3,517ft.
