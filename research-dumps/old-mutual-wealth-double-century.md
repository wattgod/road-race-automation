# Research Dump: Old Mutual Wealth Double Century

## Quick Facts
- Current official name (2026): Old Mutual Wealth Double Century — confirmed on the live 2026 organizer homepage
- 2026 edition: Saturday, 21 November 2026, Swellendam, Western Cape — confirmed on official homepage and Pedal Power Association (PPA) event calendar
- Format: staggered-start **team time trial**, 6-12 riders per team (12 customary max, 6 minimum; the 6th rider across the line sets the official team time)
- Advertised distance: 202 km (organizer's consistently marketed figure); the embedded official Strava route file for the current loop measures 204.5 km — treat 202 km as the marketed value and 204.5 km as the GPX-measured value, not confirmation of the previously-filed 202.8 km figure
- Elevation: officially described as "approximately 2,100 m"; the embedded mapped route calculates 2,192 m; contemporary race coverage rounds to "~2,200 m"
- Founded: 1993 (inaugural "DC" with 385 starters, per official heritage history — informal 200 km rides preceding it in the early 1990s were not organized editions)
- Field size: organizer states "3,000+" riders typically; 2024 announcement specified 3,100 cyclists; 2026 cap not yet published
- 2026 entry fee: not yet published as of research date (official entry page says "coming soon"); last verified public pricing is 2023 (R13,250 PPA-member team / R15,900 general / R17,800 corporate) — historical only, do not present as current
- Organizing bodies: Pedal Power Association (PPA), with Golazo also named on the 2026 homepage

## Sponsor-Name History (dedupe-critical)
This is ONE continuous event since 1993 under a series of sponsor-era names — not separate events:
| Period | Name used | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| 1993 onward | Double Century / DC | Official heritage history |
| ~2003-2006 | Pedal Power Double Century / PPA Double Century | Archived timing records |
| 2007-2019 | Coronation Double Century (Coronation Fund Managers, an investment manager — not a bank) | 2007 sponsor announcement; 2019 timing records |
| 2020 | Pedal Power Association Double Century (sponsor-free, COVID-reduced 188 km, 10-rider teams) | 2020 timing record; Discovery event description |
| 2021-present | Old Mutual Wealth Double Century | 2021 title-sponsor announcement; PPA Sept 2025 CEO report confirms sponsorship renewal; live 2026 homepage |

No verified "bank" sponsor phase was found (Coronation Fund Managers is an asset manager, not a bank). The 2021 edition was cancelled on race morning due to flooding/rockfalls/rain — a weather cancellation, not a series discontinuation; racing resumed under the Old Mutual Wealth name in 2022.

**Aliases for the same event**: Double Century, DC, Pedal Power Double Century, PPA Double Century, Pedal Power Association Double Century, Coronation Double Century, CDC, Coronation Double Century presented by Cycle Lab, Old Mutual Double Century, OMWDC.

## Course Profile
- Circular loop start/finish in Swellendam: Swellendam -> N2 -> Suurbraak -> R324/Tradouw Pass -> R62/Op de Tradouw (passing Barrydale) -> Montagu -> Cogmanskloof Pass -> Ashton -> R60 toward Robertson -> Bonnievale/R317 -> Rooibrug and Drew -> R60 and the final three climbs -> Van Eeden Street finish, Swellendam
- Final climb trio: officially named the "Three Stooges" on the organizer's own route page. "Three Witches" is rider vernacular (appears in a first-person YouTube recap already in this profile's youtube_data) but is not the official route-page name — the JSON should use "Three Stooges" as primary, with "Three Witches" noted as the rider nickname
- Surface: paved public roads throughout; the 2026 homepage warns of active roadworks, and an older route page documented a temporarily un-tarred construction section — not a permanent gravel claim, but not "perfect pavement guaranteed" either
- Minimum age: 19 in the event year, per latest published (2025) entry terms — 2026-specific terms not yet published
