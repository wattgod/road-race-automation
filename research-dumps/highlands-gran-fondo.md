## 6. Cutoff policy
Confirmed accurate, should be expanded:
- Gran/long-route riders must maintain greater than 10 mph progression speed
- Progression speed includes all stopped time — it is not a moving average
- Enforcement occurs at predetermined course splits
- Riders below the threshold are redirected onto a shorter route
- Organizer states "No exceptions"
- Separately, timed sections require staying within a 10-23 mph progression window to be timed/award-eligible
Source: official race page, accessed 24 July 2026: https://www.granfondonationalseries.com/gran-fondo-highlands
