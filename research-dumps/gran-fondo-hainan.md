# Research Dump: Gran Fondo Hainan

Wave 6 / 2026-07-24. Researched via codex gpt-5.6-sol web search.

## Quick Facts
- Status: **Active**, confirmed on the UCI Gran Fondo World Series 2026-2027 qualifier calendar.
- 2026 dates: December 18-20, 2026 (Friday-Sunday). Source: UCI Gran Fondo World Series calendar (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/calendar/).
- The organizer site (ucigranfondo.cn) has not yet rolled over from 2025 content as of 2026-07-24 — still shows Dec 12-14 2025 dates/fees. 2026 registration, fees, and technical route details are therefore not yet published.
- Location: Lingao, Danzhou/Yangpu, and Wuzhishan, Hainan Island, China.
- Founded: 2025 (as the UCI-branded three-day umbrella event). The Day 3 Wuzhishan queen stage absorbs/co-brands a local event with lineage back to 2023 ("Cadillac Cup TREK100 Wuzhishan Rainforest Road Cycling Challenge") — this is a constituent-event absorption, not evidence Gran Fondo Hainan itself predates 2025.

## Course Profile (2025 inaugural edition — verified)
Three independently-registered, independently-priced days, **not** a single cumulative-GC stage race in the classic sense:
- Day 1 (Dec 12, 2025): Lingao Wenlan Cultural Park → Danzhou/Yangpu Binhai Cultural Square, Hainan Coastal Scenic Highway. **110.40 km, 557 m gain.** Flat coastal sprinter's course. (UCI pre-race article rounded to 108 km; organizer page + post-race UCI report say 110.40 km.)
- Day 2 (ITT): Yangpu Economic Development Zone / Yangpu Coastal Cultural Park. **19.40 km.** Flat, exposed to coastal wind; 4.5 km approach + 5 km loop ridden twice + return. No published elevation gain.
- Day 3 (queen stage): Wuzhishan Sanyuesan Square → rainforest circuit → Atuoling summit. **80.74 km, 1,644 m gain** (post-race figure — see discrepancy note below). Early rise ~300m→755m, long descent to ~200m, then 13.4 km @ 4.1% to the 787m Atuoling finish.
- **Weekend total: 210.54 km / 130.8 mi, ≥2,201 m documented climbing** (557m Day 1 + 1,644m Day 3; TT gain unpublished).
- Surface: paved roads/highways and mountain roads throughout. No gravel/off-road component documented.
- Operationally: separate registration/pricing per day, separate qualifier results per day, riders could enter only one day, no conventional cumulative GC found in results. Best described as a three-day UCI gran-fondo race program formatted as stages with independently-enterable competitions, closer to `multi_stage` discipline than a mandatory Tour-style GC race.

### Elevation discrepancy (Day 3 queen stage) — real, not resolved
- April 2025 UCI announcement: 1,535 m
- Dec 11, 2025 UCI preview: 1,236 m (two-part 15km climb to 729m, finish ~785m)
- Dec 14, 2025 post-race report + local China News Service report: **1,644 m, finish 787m** — best available final value.

### Field size and fees (2025)
- ~2,300 aggregate stage entries from 34 countries (Day 1: 907, ITT: 392, Day 3: 1,023 — sums to 2,322). This is aggregate stage participation, not a confirmed unique-rider count.
- 2025 fees (sold per-day): Day 1 $40/$55/$80 early-mid-late; ITT $35/$45/$60; Day 3 $40/$55/$80; all three bundled $115/$155/$220. 5+ rider team discount of $5/rider. 2026 fees not yet published.

## On-file data error
The prior on-file vitals (109.4 km / 1,219 m) do not match any single stage, the weekend total, or any published combination. 109.4 km is close to but not equal to the verified Day 1 distance (110.40 km); 1,219 m is close to but not equal to the preliminary Day 3 announcement (1,236 m), and doesn't match the final 1,644 m Day 3 figure or a weekend total. No official source publishes 109.4 km and 1,219 m together — this pairing appears to be a data-entry/migration error, not a genuine stage figure.

## Identity Check
Confirmed as the real, current event:
- UCI competition record: Gran Fondo Hainan, Lingao/Danzhou/Wuzhishan, Dec 12-14 2025, class UGF (UCI Gran Fondo World Series). https://www.uci.org/competition-details/2025/CPT/76670
- Organizer (ucigranfondo.cn) publishes real physical start venues, road courses, registration, age-group results, World Championship qualifier status. Not a motorcycle race, not a Zwift/virtual event.
- **Not the Tour of Hainan** — that is a separate men's pro UCI ProSeries race (class 2.Pro), held April 15-19 2026, five stages, cumulative GC. Do not conflate.
- **The prior eligibility source (CycloWorld, cycloworld.cc) is contaminated/wrong**: it claims Dec 13, 2026, Lingao-Wanning, 174 km/205 m — every substantive detail (date, route endpoint, distance, elevation) conflicts with the official UCI listing. Do not use CycloWorld for this event's status or course data; it appears to describe a different Hainan road event entirely.
