## Relocation/rebrand ground truth
On Jan 15, 2026 the UCI series announced the Portuguese round, originally slated for Coimbra (Feb 28-Mar 1), had been "officially relocated and rescheduled" to Leiria/Pombal (Mar 28-29) under the new name Granfondo Leiria Region – Sicó Lands, with the same organizer (Cabreira Solutions). Local press (Diário de Leiria, Jan 21 2026) independently describes the same continuity: after five years in Coimbra, the race changed name and territory, with the Leiria regional partnership intended to run three years. No credible evidence of an independent Coimbra-branded race continuing in 2026+; stale third-party listings recycling the old name should not be trusted.
