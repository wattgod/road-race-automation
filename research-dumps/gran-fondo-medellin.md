# Research Dump: Gran Fondo Medellín / UCI Gran Fondo Colombia

## Quick Facts
- Confirmed upcoming event, officially branded UCI Gran Fondo Colombia, held in Medellín on 29 November 2026
- No distinct 2026 event officially named plain "Gran Fondo Medellín" was found
- Confirmed vitals: 120 km; just under 2,000 m climbing; one confirmed route/qualifier distance (120 km)
- File discrepancies corrected: -20 km (was 140 km), roughly -370 m elevation (was 2,370 m), and November (not October TBD)
- Course: starts in central Medellín on Avenida del Ferrocarril at 1,480 m, climbs immediately through Antioquia's Andean terrain to 2,525 m by km 21, then descends toward Rionegro, through Guarne toward Bello, and back into Medellín (17 km descent) with a flat 13 km finish
- Genuine UCI Gran Fondo World Series qualifier — all participants timed by age group, top 25% of starters in each category qualify for the UCI World Championships
- The event's own website still contains stale Bogotá wording despite announcing Medellín, reducing confidence in operational details
- The event is a Medellín relocation of Gran Fondo Colombia — not clearly a continuation of the 2022 "Gran Fondo Medellín" associated with Nairo Quintana, which covered roughly 150 km

## Identity note
This profile previously described an event dated "October TBD" at 140 km/2,370 m associated loosely with Nairo Quintana. Research confirms a real, currently scheduled UCI Gran Fondo Colombia event in Medellín for 29 November 2026 at 120 km — but does NOT confirm this is the same event/lineage as the earlier ~150 km Nairo Quintana-associated race. Editorial content updated to describe the confirmed UCI event with an explicit, honest disclosure of this identity uncertainty rather than asserting continuity that isn't independently verified.
