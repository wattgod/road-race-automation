## Claim: "grown into a five-event national series spanning Greenville, Chattanooga, Lehigh Valley, Bentonville, and Merced"
- **Verified true.** All five cities confirmed as current/recent Gran Fondo Hincapie series stops via hincapie.com's own event pages:
  - Greenville, SC — flagship event, 15th anniversary, Oct 17, 2026 (https://hincapie.com/pages/gran-fondo-greenville)
  - Chattanooga, TN — May 3, 2025 edition confirmed (https://hincapie.com/pages/gran-fondo-chattanooga)
  - Lehigh Valley, PA — May 31, 2025 edition confirmed (https://hincapie.com/pages/gran-fondo-lehigh-valley)
  - Bentonville, AR — confirmed as a host city ("first time the company has been to the cycling Mecca of the country") (https://hincapie.com/blogs/in-the-media/gran-fondo-hincapie-makes-first-visit-to-bentonville)
  - Merced, CA — confirmed as "the only West Coast and gravel event of the US gran fondo series" (referenced via hincapie.com site search)
- Series expansion history corroborated: Greenville was the original event (founded 2012); Chattanooga added as first expansion for the inaugural May 5, 2018 edition, per Rich Hincapie's statement on standardizing event formats across cities. https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/2999/gran-fondo-hincapie-expands-events-with-an-aim-to-standardize-route-distances-and-the-highend-customer-experience
